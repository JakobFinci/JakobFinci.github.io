    About Me

    Hello! I'm Elias (Eliyahu) Suskind, a passionate creator and problem-solver. With a strong foundation in both the technical and creative aspects of projects, I specialize in combining hardware, software, and machine learning to craft innovative solutions. I am deeply committed to exploring new interdisciplinary fields and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

    My academic journey has led me through numerous fields, from product development at Babson to Electrical and Computer Engineering at Olin College all the way to now where I am studying Biological Sciences at ASU and soon to enroll in an MS CS or ECE program (still deciding on where to enroll!). I'm also passionate about the arts, having worked with various media, including photography and music, to express ideas and evoke emotions.

    In my free time, I enjoy geocaching, hiking in the Cascades, and experimenting with new technologies. I believe in lifelong learning and continuously seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills in creative and technical domains.
