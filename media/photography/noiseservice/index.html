    Noise Service Album Release Party

    A raw and emotive collection of photographs capturing the night Noise Service released their debut album. Founded in 2021 in Seattle, WA, Noise Service has become a standout in the Seattle indie scene with their kaleidoscopic fusion rock. Musicians Andy Mowdy, Corey Ray Zimmerman, Katharine McAllister, and Rolando Sotomayor Pino serve concert-goers a vibrant experience with powerful drumming, on-stage chemistry, wild guitar solos, and psychedelic effects. Fans recognize shoegaze, retro rock, and Latin influence in Noise Service. Unusual song structures and melodic storytelling generate the Noise Service experience.
